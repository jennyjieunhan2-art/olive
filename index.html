Our Philosophy

Holistic Healing for Body, Mind & Spirit

"We don't just treat symptoms — we address the root cause."

At Olive Acupuncture clinic in Houston, Texas, we follow a holistic approach to healthcare. We firmly believe in the body's inherent capacity to heal itself. Our objective is to provide support and enhance this natural healing process through acupuncture and other holistic therapies.

Traditional Chinese Medicine considers the body a complex system controlled and balanced by individual organs and energetic pathways, or meridians. We are dedicated not only to alleviating the symptoms that brought you to us but also to addressing any underlying health issues impacting your overall well-being.

Proudly serving patients from Houston, Westchase, Alief, Sugar Land, and surrounding areas.

Our Treatments

Comprehensive therapies rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine, tailored to your unique health goals and concerns.

Acupuncture

Using the twelve meridians to balance the body's restorative Qi, acupuncture returns the body to its natural equilibrium. Immediate and long-term pain relief through precise needle placement.

Learn more →

Electro-Acupuncture

FDA-approved electrical stimulation applied to acupuncture needles. Improves healing of injured joints and muscles, increases pain-relieving endorphin release, and stimulates balanced nervous system function.

Learn more →

Cupping Therapy

Small glass or plastic cups create suction on your skin to stimulate circulation in superficial muscle layers. Effective for arthritis, musculoskeletal pain, headaches, colds, and coughs.

Learn more →

Moxibustion

Therapeutic heat therapy using Mugwort and other herbs, applied directly or indirectly to acupuncture points. Especially effective for cold, achy joints, scar tissue, and immune system support.

Learn more →

Gua Sha

A traditional Chinese healing method using a smooth-edged tool to stroke the skin, promoting circulation and releasing tension. Raises petechiae under the skin to signal improved blood flow.

Learn more →

Auricular Acupuncture

The auricle of the ear maps to the entire human body. Ear needles, seeds, or magnets stimulate points to relieve pain, support addiction recovery, PTSD, insomnia, and stress management.

Learn more →

Meet Your Practitioner

John Han, L.Ac.

Master of Oriental Medicine · South Baylo University

Growing up in South Korea, John was immersed in the rich cultural traditions of Oriental medicine, igniting a lifelong passion for this ancient healing art. He pursued his degree at South Baylo University in Anaheim, California, earning his Master's degree in Oriental Medicine and earning licensure through the California Acupuncture Board.

After completing his studies, John returned to Korea to deepen his practical experience and joined a non-profit acupuncture association, volunteering services to underprivileged communities. Since 2008, John has been providing effective treatments across Southern California and now brings his expertise to Houston, Texas.

With Olive Acupuncture Clinic, John is dedicated to compassionate, patient-centered care — helping individuals achieve optimal health and enhance their quality of life through holistic Oriental medicine.
